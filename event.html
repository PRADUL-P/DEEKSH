RULES

&Regulations

○ The drawings should be made on an art paper or an ordinary paper as per the convenience of the participant.

○ There is no restriction for usage of colours.

○ A good quality photograph of the drawing is to be submitted in jpeg/png format.

○ A participant can send only one entry for a Category.

○ The Cartoon should be the work of the participant and not copied. It should not contain any third party

○ materials and/or that which violates any copyrights.

○ Drawings received after the cut-off date and time will not be considered for evaluation.
